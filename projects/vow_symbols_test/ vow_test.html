Vow of the Disciple - Symbols Test

        The Destiny 2 raid 'Vow of the Disciple' features mechanics revolving around ~27 symbols. Being able to quickly identify these is a huge part of successfully completing this raid. With that in mind, this symbols test serves as a quick game to test your memory of these symbols.

        The symbols and responses are drawn at random and the difficulty increases in 2 tiers, one at 5 points and the next at 10. If the timer reaches 0 or you miss an answer, the difficulty will reduce to its default, giving more time and fewer false options. Hope this helps! Happy raiding, Guardian!
